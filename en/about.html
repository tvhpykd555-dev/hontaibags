About Hongtai

Yangzhou, Jiangsu · 20+ Years of Bag Manufacturing

Company Profile

Jiangsu Hongtai Bags Co., Ltd., formerly Huayang Travel Products Co., Ltd., was founded in 2003. With over 20 years of specialized experience in bag manufacturing, we now operate three factories located in Yizheng (Yangzhou), Guangling (Yangzhou), and Suining (Xuzhou), Jiangsu Province.

The company employs approximately 200 staff, including about 80 skilled sewing workers. Our facilities are equipped with automatic cutting machines, laser cutting machines, automatic template machines, and dozens of various sewing machines, providing full production capabilities from sampling to mass production.

We have long served Japanese market clients including Takashimaya, Dentsu, Unifast, Takakoh, Rikisho, and i-goods, while also manufacturing for international brand Herschel and domestic brand Laura Star. End clients include DHC, Suntory, Kirin, and Asahi.
