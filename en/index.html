About Us
Yangzhou, Jiangsu · 20+ Years of Bag Manufacturing

Jiangsu Hongtai Bags Co., Ltd., formerly Huayang Travel Products Co., Ltd., established in 2003. With over 20 years of experience, we now operate three factories in Yizheng, Guangling, and Suining, Jiangsu Province. We employ approximately 200 staff with advanced cutting and sewing equipment.

We serve Japanese trading companies (Takashimaya, Dentsu, Unifast) and international brands (Herschel, Laura Star). End clients include DHC, Suntory, Kirin, Asahi, and more. Sampling time: 3-7 days. Monthly output: 8,000-10,000 pieces. No minimum order quantity.
